Who are we?

Indian Construction Network is a company that is dedicated to providing high-quality construction and related services to customers across India. Our team of four Civil Engineering students has developed a platform that connects customers with the right products and services at the best prices. We believe that our mission of creating a sustainable and equitable future for the construction industry can make a positive impact on the lives of our customers and vendors. Our platform provides a space for small-scale vendors to reach a wider audience and compete fairly with larger-scale vendors. Additionally, customers can access a range of products and services that meet their needs and budget.

At Indian Construction Network, we offer a range of services to our customers. Our platform offers building construction materials, machinery, and construction professionals, as well as basic services such as plumbing, electrical work, and painting, all performed by skilled certified professionals who are vetted for quality and safety. We strive to provide the best services from all sectors, including basic necessities, households, rentals, and emergency services. We are committed to providing exceptional customer service and support, and our team is available to answer any questions or concerns customers may have about our platform or services. Join us on the Indian Construction Network and become part of a community dedicated to building a better future for the construction industry. Together, we can create a more sustainable and equitable ecosystem that benefits everyone.
